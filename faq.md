4. FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions)
   Page Title: Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
   A. Registration & Account
   Q: How do I register?

   18+.
   B. Deposits
   Q: How can I deposit?

   Money/Wallets (like Opay, Palmpay, Moniepoint).
   Q: Is there a minimum deposit?
   A: Yes, the minimum deposit is ₦100.
   Q: My deposit didn't reflect instantly. What should I do?
   A: Contact our 24/7 support via live chat or WhatsApp. Have your transaction reference handy.
   C. Bonuses & Promotions
   Q: What is a wagering requirement?
   A: It's the number of times you must play through a bonus amount before you can withdraw
   winnings from it. E.g., A ₦10,000 bonus with a 30x requirement means you must bet ₦300,000
   before withdrawal.
   Q: Can I withdraw my bonus immediately?
   A: No. You must meet the wagering requirements stated in the specific promotion's terms.
   D. Withdrawals
   Q: How do I withdraw my winnings?
   A: Go to the "Wallet," select "Withdraw," choose your preferred method, and enter the amount.
   Your first withdrawal requires account verification.
   Q: How long do withdrawals take?
   A: Verified withdrawals are typically processed within 1-2 hours. It may take longer due to third
   party payment processors or your bank.
   Q: Is there a withdrawal limit?
   A: Yes. The maximum daily withdrawal is ₦1,000,000. Please check the "Withdrawals" section
   in the Terms for full details.
   E. Sports Betting
   Q: What games can I bet on?

   Card games, Penalty shootout, Spinning wheel games and more.
   F. Responsible Gambling
   Q: How can I gamble responsibly?
   A: Set deposit limits in your account, take regular breaks, and never chase losses. Contact
   www.gamblealert.org for support.
   G. Security & Fairness
